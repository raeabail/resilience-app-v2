Avon Park Sentinel Landscape

Expanded case study describing objectives, methods, outcomes, and lessons learned.

Overview

The Avon Park case study focuses on aquifer protection and habitat restoration in south central Florida. The scorecard informed wetland restoration priorities that balance military readiness and ecological conservation.

Methods

We used hydrology, land cover, and connectivity metrics to prioritize restoration sites that maximize ecological benefit and resilience.

Results & Outcomes

  • Prioritized restoration sites for wetland connectivity
  • Highlighted parcels where protection prevents fragmentation
  • Produced project-ready GIS layers and recommendations
