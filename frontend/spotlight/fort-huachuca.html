Fort Huachuca Sentinel Landscape

Expanded case study describing objectives, methods, outcomes, and lessons learned.

Overview

The Fort Huachuca study examines a high-desert landscape where water conservation and wildfire mitigation are priorities. The Resilience Scorecard helped identify priority areas for fuel breaks and restoration.

Methods

We used spatial layers for vegetation, fuel loading, and topography, combined with land use priorities, to quantify resilience and guide management actions.

Results & Outcomes

  • Mapped priority zones for fuel reduction
  • Suggested restoration placements to protect infrastructure
  • Provided GIS-ready outputs for agency partners
